Meet Maulik Patel

Math & Science Teacher | Outdoor Learning Enthusiast | Lifelong Educator

About Maulik

Maulik Patel (He/Him) began his journey in education teaching Engineering Mathematics in India. After moving to Canada, he transitioned through business management and retail training back into education. Today, he teaches Math and Science to intermediate and senior students in Ontario, creating inclusive, engaging spaces where learning thrives.

Teaching Philosophy

Learning should be authentic, inclusive, and connected to the real world. Maulik focuses on building trusting environments, linking curriculum to life beyond school, and encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration in every lesson.

Why Outdoor Experiential Education?

Maulik believes the best lessons often happen beyond four walls. Through the Outdoor Experiential Education course, he hopes to gain strategies for creating meaningful outdoor learning experiences that teach resilience, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Fun Facts About Maulik

  • Former Engineering Mathematics Lecturer
  • Proud father to a lively one-year-old
  • Weekend adventurer and backyard scientist
  • Bilingual: English and Gujarati
  • Master multitasker: Lesson plans + bedtime stories!

Final Thought

"Teaching doesn't have to be confined by walls. Sometimes, the best classroom is a muddy trail, an open field, or a starry night sky."
